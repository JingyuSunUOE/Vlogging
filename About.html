Vlogging - Student Community of University of Edinburgh

We are an association of the University of Edinburgh, and most of the members are made up of our students.

We also welcome non-student members to join us if you have the same interests as us.

We have different shooting themes every month, you can use your creativity under these themes, and there are no restrictions on shooting methods.

Although we are not a professional photography team, but we have a wealth of experience, you can get any help you want here.

Our community also provides some shooting equipment, including microphones, microphones and spotlights.

All the videos taken will be uploaded to the club's media platform, and we will give appropriate rewards based on popularity.

If you are only interested in our community and don't want to join us, please give us your opinion on the video we made.

We look forward to anyone joining.
